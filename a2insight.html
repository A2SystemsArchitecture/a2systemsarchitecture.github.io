The Opportunity
Every Score Tells Part of the Story. We Find the Rest.

Two students can receive the same score for completely different reasons. One has a reasoning gap. Another has a conceptual gap. Another has a cross-disciplinary gap. Those are not the same opportunity, which means they should never receive the same support.

Most academic support begins with instruction: more tutoring, more practice, more test prep. But if the specific growth opportunity has never been correctly identified, those efforts often produce limited or temporary results. A parent who spends $150 per hour on a tutor for three months without score movement has spent $5,000 to $7,000 and still does not know exactly where their child is primed for a breakthrough.

A2 Insight answers that question before a single tutoring dollar is spent, and makes everything that follows more effective.

The Service
What Is A2 Insight?

A2 Insight is a student diagnostic service operating under A2 Systems Architecture. It applies the same institutional-grade data analysis methodology A2 uses with schools directly to individual students, giving families a precise, expert understanding of exactly where their child is primed for a breakthrough and what needs to happen next.

We do not provide tutoring. We provide the diagnostic clarity that makes all tutoring, instruction, and academic support more targeted, more efficient, and more effective. The result is not a better score report. The result is a parent who finally understands what their child needs, and a student who sees their gaps not as limitations but as a precise map of where their breakthrough lives.

How It Works
Our Process
Step 01
Data Collection

You provide available academic data: FAST scores, EOC results, SAT or ACT reports, ISEE or SSAT score reports, report cards, teacher feedback, or progress monitoring information.

Step 02
Diagnostic Analysis

Our team conducts a structured analysis of your student's academic performance patterns to pinpoint exactly where they are primed for a breakthrough and identify the specific growth opportunities the score does not show.

Step 03
Diagnostic Findings Brief

You receive a written diagnostic findings brief outlining key findings, identified growth opportunities, observed academic patterns, and recommended priority areas.

Step 04
Parent Debrief Session

We walk you through the findings in clear, direct language. You leave understanding what is happening, why it is happening, and exactly what needs to happen next.
